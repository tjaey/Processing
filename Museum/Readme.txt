 Platform Used: Processing 3.4 on macOS High Sierra, Intel HD Graphics 4000 1536 MB

 	- Museum can be navigated using keyboard:

 			w = move forward
 			s = move back
 			d = rotate right 90 degrees
 			a = rotate left 90 degrees


 	- Movement confined to path(dark ground) and within museum walls.
 	
 	- press 'r' to switch between a overhead view from the top of the musem and first-person view.


All textures used in museum and all exhibits are sourced from textures.com
